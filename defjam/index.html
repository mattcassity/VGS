Mega stardom and 30 years in music doesn't happen with just one act. For Bon Jovi’s album What About Now we collaborated with Acadamy Award winning producer Fisher Stevens to create a three-act narrative that blurs the line between music video and feature film. Each piece uses a different song off the album to create one multi-dimensional story of love, war, and rock n roll.
